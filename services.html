Acupuncture

Acupuncture is a holistic healing technique that has been used for thousands of years. It involves the insertion of thin needles or a cold laser into specific points on the body to stimulate the flow of energy and promote natural healing. This traditional Chinese medicine practice has been found to be effective in easing various types of chronic pain, including low-back pain, neck pain, and osteoarthritis/knee pain. In addition, acupuncture has been shown to reduce the frequency and severity of tension headaches and prevent migraine headaches. By balancing the body's energy flow, acupuncture can also help to reduce stress and anxiety, and promote overall relaxation and well-being.
